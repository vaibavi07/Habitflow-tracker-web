Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my habits?
Add a habit from your dashboard and click the check-in button each day you complete it.
What happens if I miss a day?
If you miss a day, your streak will reset to 0 for that habit. You can start again the next day.
Can I track multiple habits?
Yes! You can add as many habits as you want.
